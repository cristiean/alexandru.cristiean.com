Hygiene

21 May 2025

Over the years I grew entitled to people's presence, often taking it for granted. Showing up scattered and emotionally unkept, repeatedly, over a long period of time, was unhygienic for both and unfair to them.

"Isn't that's what friends are for?". Sure, but as an exception— not the rule. It's better not to abuse paracetamol or antibiotics just to get by, but to remove the triggers of one's headaches or strengthen one's immune system.

Have I done enough to look after myself before going out into the world?

Probably not.

...

Thank God for those who stuck with me regardless of my lack of regard.
